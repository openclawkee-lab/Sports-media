Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how UG Today ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our sports news publishing application ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. About the App

UG Today is a desktop application that aggregates sports news content and publishes it to social media platforms including TikTok, Facebook, YouTube, Instagram, and Telegram on behalf of the user. The App is used solely by its operator to manage their own social media accounts.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the App:

  • OAuth Access Tokens: When you connect a social media account (e.g. TikTok), the App stores your OAuth access token and refresh token locally on your device. These tokens are never shared with third parties.
  • Account Identifiers: Platform-issued user IDs (e.g. TikTok Open ID) are stored locally to identify the connected account.
  • Content Data: Sports news text, images, and video files processed through the App are used solely for publishing to your own social media accounts and are not stored on any external server.
  • Log Data: The App may record local error logs for debugging. These logs remain on your device and are not transmitted externally.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate and post content to your connected social media accounts
  • Refresh access tokens to maintain account connectivity
  • Display the status and results of publishing actions within the App

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information or credentials with any third party.

4. Third-Party Platforms

The App interacts with third-party APIs including TikTok, Facebook, YouTube, Instagram, and Telegram. When you connect these platforms, you are subject to their respective privacy policies:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Privacy Policy
  • Google (YouTube) Privacy Policy
  • Telegram Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Security

All credentials and tokens are stored locally on your own device. No data is uploaded to or stored on our servers. You are responsible for securing access to your device and App configuration files.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained on your local device for as long as you use the App. You may delete all stored credentials at any time by removing the App's configuration files or uninstalling the App.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
